About Us

Egedo is a holding company operating in the tourism and gastronomy sectors. Founded by two visionary Turkish entrepreneurs and investors. Our company is proudly based in Portugal while serving a global clientele. We are excited to announce that our operations will commence shortly, bringing exceptional services to customers worldwide.

Our mission is to blend cultural richness with outstanding quality, providing innovative solutions and unparalleled customer support in every endeavor we undertake.

Tourism

In the tourism sector, Egedo specializes in hotel and flight ticket sales, offering competitive prices, seamless customer support, and premium quality service. We provide access to flights from anywhere to anywhere worldwide, as well as hotel bookings in all major destinations globally.

Our commitment is to deliver hassle-free travel experiences with personalized assistance, ensuring our clients enjoy every journey with confidence and comfort.

Gastronomy

Our gastronomy division is set to open soon in Lisbon, offering authentic Turkish cuisine delights. We aim to bring the rich flavors of Turkey to the heart of Portugal, providing a unique and memorable dining experience.

We look forward to welcoming food enthusiasts to savor traditional recipes made with the finest ingredients and passionate craftsmanship.
